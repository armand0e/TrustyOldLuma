# Steam Family Setup

To set up a Steam Family, go to Account Details on the Store page, then click on the Family Management section.

[<img src="https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images//27766192/c6bb419cc46066b493f7c45f08c9324d155bbd33.png">](https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/054C-3167-DD7F-49D4)

- Click 'Create a Family' (or use your existing family)
- Invite the account that you wish to share your game(s) with
- The invited account will receive an alert in their steam mobile app, where they can then accept the invite.
